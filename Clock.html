Privacy Policy

For Chess Clocky (Funny Chess Clock) — Last updated: March 5, 2025

Chess Clocky (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what information we handle, how we use it, and your choices when using our mobile application.

1) Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or share personal information such as your name, email, phone number, payment details, or precise location. The app does not require account registration.

We may collect non-personal data such as device type, operating system version, and basic app usage statistics solely to improve performance and user experience. This data does not identify you.

All user content (such as saved settings, themes, or local images) is processed and stored locally on your device. We do not upload or share your files with any server or third party.

2) Permissions

The app may request access to your camera or microphone if you enable the optional “Funny Mode” with animations or sounds. These features work entirely offline and no data is uploaded or shared.

The app may also request access to storage to save settings or user-generated content, and basic internet access for updates or to load optional resources. No personal data is collected through these permissions.

You can manage or revoke permissions at any time in your device settings.

3) Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services. No user data is shared with external providers.

4) AI-Generated & External Assets

Some icons, images, or sound effects in the app may be AI-generated or royalty-free assets from legal sources (e.g., Pixabay, Mixkit, or Zapsplat). They are not intended to depict real persons, brands, or copyrighted material.

5) Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for users 18 years and older. It is not directed to children, and no data is knowingly collected from minors.

6) Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information you may share with us (for example via email support). While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Use of the app is at your own risk.

7) Your Rights & Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, contact us at devmivox@gmail.com. If you ever provide information to us (for example by contacting support), you may request access or deletion of that information via the same email.

8) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
