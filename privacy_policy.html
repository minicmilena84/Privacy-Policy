For SportVid — Last updated: September 30, 2025

SportVid (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what information we handle, how we use it, and your choices when using our mobile application.

We do not collect, store, or share personal information such as your name, email, phone number, payment details, or precise location. The app does not require account registration.

In addition, the app may request basic permissions such as storage access or internet connection strictly to provide functionality. We do not use permissions to gather personal information. You can manage and revoke permissions at any time in your device settings.

The app does not use third-party analytics or advertising services, and does not share data with third parties.

5) Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for users 18 years and older. It is not directed to children and no data is knowingly collected from minors.

6) Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information you choose to share with us (e.g., via email support). While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Use of the app is at your own risk.
