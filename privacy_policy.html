For SportVid — Last updated: September 25, 2025

SportVid (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what information we handle, how we use it, and your choices when using our mobile application.

We do not collect, store, or share personal information such as your name, email, phone number, payment details, or precise location. The app does not require account registration.

The app may request basic Android permissions (e.g., storage access or internet connection) strictly to provide functionality. We do not use permissions to gather personal information.

The app is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6) Security

7) Your Rights & Contact
